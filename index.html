🚇 Getting Around

Door-to-door from each home base. Payment differs by city — check each note. Times are estimates; confirm live on Citymapper before leaving.

🇬🇧 London · Base: Gordon Place, W8

💳 Contactless / Apple Pay works. Teddy (10) rides free with a fare-paying adult; Chloe (12) needs a child Oyster (Young Visitor discount, or an 11–15 Zip). Check Citymapper + the train's front destination before each ride.

Tower of London 30–35 min

High St Kensington → Circle line eastbound (Platform 2, front reads 'Edgware Road via Victoria') — direct to Tower Hill, no change.

Tower Hill is right at the gates. Catch the free Yeoman (Beefeater) tour early — last one 15:15.

Harry Potter Studio Tour · Wed Jul 1 60–75 min

From Sip/the flat: get to Shepherds Bush National Rail (walk/bus/short taxi depending on energy) → Southern "West London line" train DIRECT to Watford Junction (~31 min, 09:53 → 10:24, contactless tap in/out) → Studio shuttle from Watford Junction (~15–20 min).

Leave the flat ~9:15; be at Shepherds Bush by ~09:45. Show e-ticket 7156772 for the shuttle; buses run at least every 30 min.

Notting Hill / Portobello 12–15 min

12–15 min walk north, or High St Ken → Notting Hill Gate (1 stop) then walk.

Portobello market is quiet midweek (you're there Thu).

Covent Garden ~25 min

District/Circle to South Kensington → Piccadilly line direct to Covent Garden.

Eurostar · St Pancras · Fri Jul 3, 08:16 ~30 min

Primary: Uber Reserve XL / Bolt XL from Gordon Place around 05:50, ~25–35 min. Backup: Circle line High St Kensington → King's Cross St Pancras if the car flakes.

Arrive ~06:20–06:30. EES/biometric kiosks and security happen before boarding; ticket gates close before departure.

Scenic No. 11 bus (cheap top-deck tour) 45–60 min

Get to Sloane Square → bus 11 toward Waterloo; sit upstairs, front. Passes Victoria, Westminster/Parliament, County Hall.

£1.75 contactless; Teddy free on buses. Route now ends at Waterloo (older St Paul's advice is outdated).

🇳🇱 Amsterdam · Base: Park Plaza Vondelpark, Koningslaan 3

💳 OVpay contactless — one card/device per rider, tap in AND out. Much of the centre is walkable straight through Vondelpark.

Amsterdam Centraal → Park Plaza Vondelpark · Fri Jul 3 arrival 15–25 min

With 6 bags: taxi/Uber/Bolt Van from the station rank/app to Koningslaan 3 (~€20–25). Budget backup: tram 2 to Cornelis Schuytstraat, then 5-min walk.

Hotel check-in is 15:00; desk can hold bags before check-in. Call +31 20 262 6874 if you want to ask about early check-in.

Anne Frank House · Sat Jul 4, 10:00 25–30 min

~2 km walk NE through Vondelpark + the canal ring to Westermarkt 20 (foolproof & scenic). To shorten: tram 2 (dir Centraal) from Cornelis Schuytstraat to Leidseplein + 12-min walk.

Westermarkt tram stop is closed (trackworks to 2028). No large bags — leave luggage at the hotel.

Park Plaza → Amsterdam Centraal · Sun Jul 5, 16:10 Eurostar 15–25 min

With luggage, use Bolt/Uber/taxi Van from the hotel around 15:00; aim to reach Centraal by ~15:30. Tram 2 direct is the cheap/scenic backup if bags are manageable.

Amsterdam→Paris is Schengen-internal: no passport control; scan QR at station gates. Eurostar says arrive at least 20 min early; 30–40 min is a safer family buffer.

Around the centre / canal cruise —

Tram 2 (dir Centraal) from Cornelis Schuytstraat toward Leidseplein & the centre; much of the centre is walkable via Vondelpark.

🇫🇷 Paris · Base: near Solférino, 7th (metro Solférino L12 · RER C Musée d'Orsay, 5-min walk)

💳 Metro/RER needs tickets loaded via the Île-de-France Mobilités app / Navigo Easy / station machines — bank-card tap-in does NOT work at the gates. Buy before you go.

Gare du Nord → Solférino flat · Sun Jul 5 arrival 20–35 min

Bolt Van booked: pickup window 19:50–20:00 at Place Napoléon III / main entrance, to near Solférino in the 7th. Use the official taxi rank as backup if the train is badly delayed.

Amsterdam→Paris has no arrival passport control. Match driver name + plate before loading bags; ignore station ride touts.

Eiffel Tower 10–30 min

25–30 min scenic walk along the Seine, or RER C from Musée d'Orsay direct to Champ de Mars–Tour Eiffel.

Summit booked Mon Jul 6, 20:00 — 'Visitors with tickets' gate, 15–20 min security, bring ID for the kids and avoid big bags.

Louvre 12–15 min

12–15 min walk across Pont Royal, or Line 12 → Concorde → Line 1 → Palais Royal–Musée du Louvre.

Booked Mon Jul 6, 14:00; Chloe & Teddy under-18 free — bring IDs/proof of age for the kids. No large bags.

Montmartre / Sacré-Cœur 20–25 min

Line 12 DIRECT: Solférino → Abbesses, then walk or take the funicular up the hill.

One-seat ride, no changes — the easiest trip of the trip.

Disneyland Paris · Wed Jul 8 ~60 min

Line 12 → Concorde → Line 1 → Châtelet → RER A (dir Marne-la-Vallée–Chessy) to the end of the line.

Pre-buy/load a Metro-Train-RER ticket valid all zones (airports excluded) before you enter; arrive before gates open; station is a 2-min walk from the park.

CDG airport · Fri Jul 10, flight 13:10 ~50 min

Primary: Bolt/Uber Van around 09:00 from the flat. Backup: regulated Paris taxi to CDG from the Left Bank is €65 fixed. RER C → St-Michel → RER B is the public-transit backup.

For the RER buy the Paris Region ↔ Airports ticket — normal Metro-Train-RER tickets exclude CDG airport stations.
